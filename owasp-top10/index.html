ℹ️ Precise Data Source & Gathering Methodology:

For the 2025 cycle, the OWASP project expanded its empirical scope significantly, analyzing security data gathered from over 2,800,000 audited applications globally between 2021 and 2024. The metrics were contributed by leading application security vendors, consultancies, bug bounty frameworks, and corporate security teams.

📊 Weighted Scoring Formula

Placement is computed through a standardized risk factor formula combining incidence and testing telemetry with community survey priority adjustments:

Risk Score = (Max Incidence Rate * 1000) + (Max Coverage * 100) + (Avg Exploit * 10) + (Avg Impact * 20) + (Sum Occurrences / 10000)
⚙️ Normalization & Mapping

Data was normalized between Human-assisted Tooling (HaT) (scanner telemetry) and Tool-assisted Humans (TaH) (manual pen testing findings) to ensure fair scaling. Across the dataset, 248 Common Weakness Enumerations (CWEs) were analyzed and mapped directly to these 10 core categories.
